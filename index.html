About Me

TLDR for recruiters: years of experience. Started in python, Took AP Comp Sci Principles, learned JavaScript, scored a 4 on the AP Exam, took AP Comp Sci A, learned Java and scored a 5 on the AP Exam. I spend much of my free time programming

My name is Samuel Hagen, I have been programming for years. I started programming in 2018 with python, making several little games to challenge myself and expand my skillset. These games included, but were not limited to, Dreidel, BlackJack, and a text-based survival game inspired by Minecraft. Sadly, most of these projects have been lost, and many others were never completed due to burn out from my lack of technical skills. I often would create bugs that were difficult to solve. Despite my failures, I always kept on coding.

In 2021, I took AP Computer Science Principles which introduced me to several key concepts of computer science. Concepts included: binary, data compression, big O notation, fault tolerance and more. We programmed in JavaScript on code.org's app lab. In the spring, I scored a 4 on the AP exam

The following year, I enrolled in AP Computer Science A where we learned Java. It was that year that I decided to major in computer science in college.
